## Welcome to the Southern Illinois Nordic Circle

The Southern Illinois Nordic Circle is a club spanning Southern Illinois for those wishing to explore and preserve the cultural heritage of the Nordic countries: Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden.
